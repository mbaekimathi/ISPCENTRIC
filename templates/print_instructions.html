HOW TO SETUP YOUR ROUTER

  1. Connect the cable from our network to your router's WAN or Internet port.
  2. Open your router settings in a web browser (usually http://192.168.1.1 or http://192.168.0.1).
  3. Find "Internet" or "WAN" settings.
  4. Change Connection Type to "PPPoE".
  5. Enter the username and password shown above.
  6. Click "Connect" or "Save".
  7. Wait 1-2 minutes for connection.

Your internet should now be working.
